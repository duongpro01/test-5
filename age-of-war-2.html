Age of War 2 stands as a timeless gem that has captivated players of all ages for years. This popular flash game has now been brought to life in the online gaming realm, offering players an immersive experience that transcends generations. Step into a world where you can train a vast and eclectic army, from cavemen riding dinosaurs to commanding powerful World War tanks and futuristic robot warriors. With seven unique ages of war and 29 distinct unit types at your disposal, including Assault Spartans, Anubis Warriors, Mages, Knights, and more, Age of War 2 takes you on an epic journey through time, all while challenging your strategic prowess.

Age of War 2 transports players through seven distinct ages, each with its own unique charm and challenges. The game's design beautifully encapsulates these ages, making every moment a visually appealing and engaging experience. From the primitive times of cavemen to the highly advanced technological era of futuristic robots, this game covers it all.

  1. The Stone Age: Begin your journey in the Stone Age, where you will lead a troop of cavemen riding dinosaurs. Unleash your creativity and tactical skills as you fend off enemy attacks.

  2. The Medieval Age: Progressing through history, you'll find yourself in the Medieval Age, where Knights and Mages take the forefront. Build your forces wisely, strategize, and engage in battles that test your wits.

  3. World War Age: The historical significance of World War I and World War II comes to life in this age. Tanks, machine guns, and military strategies define the battlefield as you work to conquer your enemies.

  4. The Future Age: Take a leap into the future, where you'll harness the power of technology and command armies of futuristic robot warriors. The high-tech warfare in this age is nothing short of thrilling.

Age of War 2 offers a wide array of 29 different unit types that can be recruited into your army. These units add layers of depth to the gameplay, ensuring that no two battles are alike. Here are a few notable units:

  1. Assault Spartans: These formidable warriors bring strength and resilience to your army, making them a formidable force on the battlefield.

  2. Anubis Warriors: Channel the powers of ancient Egypt with these mystical and enigmatic warriors. They bring a unique edge to your army's composition.

  3. Mages: Tap into the power of magic as you command Mages to cast spells and turn the tide of battle in your favor.

  4. Knights: The chivalrous Knights represent an era of honor and valor. Use their strength and bravery to protect your base.

To fortify your defenses, Age of War 2 provides players with the option to build turrets. Among these, the Chicken Turret stands out as an iconic and humorous favorite. These turrets add an extra layer of strategy to the game, allowing you to fend off enemy forces and protect your base effectively.

Age of War 2 brings together the best offense and a formidable defense, creating epic clashes that keep players on their toes. It's a game where strategy and adaptability are key to success. Whether you're fending off waves of enemies or launching an all-out assault, every moment in the game is packed with action and excitement.
